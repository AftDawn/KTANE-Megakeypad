On the Subject of Megakeypad

Twenty-four? TWENTY-FOUR!?

The module contains 24 buttons each with a symbol. The 4 2x3 subsections of the grid (as seen below) each contain two groups of three symbols that need to be pressed in reading order of the table below, and whose individual symbols need to be pressed in order from left to right as they appear in the table. Pressing a symbol out of order will record a strike and clear your entire input.
